About Us

We are a modern barbershop in the beautiful city of Rotterdam. With great quality, friendly barbers and a user friendly service, we provide a unique place for you to get fresh. We know that time is precious and therefore believe it is important to make an appointment quickly and easily. In a few simple steps you can schedule your appointment online. That means no waiting time. Book online, choose one of our barbers and sit back, relax and get fresh.
